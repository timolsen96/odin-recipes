Chili con Carné

Description

This is a recipe for the best homemade (basic) chili con carne that doesn't cost a lot to make and will leave you full with plenty of leftovers.

Ingredients

  • 2 white onions
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 1 can of chopped tomatoes
  • 1 can of kidneys beans
  • 1 can of baked beans
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 400g of ground beef
  • Half a jar of jalapeños
  • Chili seasoning

Steps

  1. Chop the onions and garlic
  2. Heat a pot with olive oil and add the onions and garlic. Cook until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add the ground beef to the pot and brown it.
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, kidney beans, baked beans and tomato paste to the pot. Stir it around.
  5. Add 1-2 deciliter of water depending on texture. Start with 1, you can always add more.
  6. Add your chili seasoning.
  7. Add the jalapeños
  8. Stir for 30-45 minutes on low-medium heat. The longer the chili cooks, the more flavor it gets.
