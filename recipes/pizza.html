Pizza

Description

This is a recipe on how to go from having no pizza in your vicinity to having pizza in your vicinity.

Ingredients

  • A functioning smartphone with access to the Internet
  • 2 beers

Steps

  1. Go to the App store and download Just Eat
  2. Open Just Eat and find a place that makes decent looking pizza
  3. Order the pizza
  4. Drink 1 beer while you wait for the pizza to arrive
  5. Drink another beer while eating the pizza
  6. Profit
